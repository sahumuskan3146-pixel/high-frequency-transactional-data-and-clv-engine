# High Frequency Data & CLV (Customer Lifetime Value) Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview
This enterprise-grade Power BI dashboard transforms high-frequency transactional data into actionable financial metrics, specifically targeting Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) and risk monitoring. Designed for executive decision-making, it bridges the gap between raw transaction anomalies and long-term corporate revenue sustainability.

## 🛠️ Core Tech Stack & Financial Analyst Skills
*   **Power BI & DAX (Advanced Financial Visualization):** Formulated complex DAX measures to compute running totals, customer recency, purchase frequency, and predictive monetary values.
*   **Data Modeling (Star Schema):** Structured transactional tables and customer master files into an optimized relational data model to handle high-frequency rows without latency.
*   **Statistics (Predictive CLV & Anomaly Detection):** Applied statistical distribution analysis to establish baseline transaction behaviors and isolate a 84.174% anomaly/fraud rate threshold.
*   **Financial Modeling (Cohort & Retention Strategy):** Segmented customer behaviors to allocate marketing capital efficiently, balancing Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) against long-term financial ROI.

## 📈 Key Visual Insights & Features
*   **Executive Financial Overview:** Real-time summary displaying **$112.82M Total Predicted CLV**, isolating core revenue potential and long-term valuation trends.
*   **Customer Deep-Dive Analytics:** Interactive matrices plotting transaction frequency against monetary value to immediately flag "At-Risk" segments vs "Champions".
*   **Risk & Anomaly Monitoring:** Dedicated risk visualizer isolating a **84.174% Anomaly/Fraud Rate**, indicating exactly where periodic manual audits are required to protect margins.
*   **Strategic Budget Allocation Model:** A data-driven framework built directly into the insights panel to optimize financial resources (e.g., allocating 70% retention budget to high-value cohorts).

## 💡 Financial Impact & ROI
*   **Capital Optimization:** Replaced generic marketing spend with a segmented retention strategy, maximizing ROI by focusing capital on high-frequency, high-monetary cohorts.
*   **Risk Mitigation:** Early detection of high-frequency transactional anomalies protects corporate cash flow from leakage and operational fraud.
